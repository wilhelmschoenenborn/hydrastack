Warranty, in one paragraph

Every stainless module — lids, mids, and unpowered bases — is covered for life against manufacturing defects: failed welds, thread defects, or insulation loss. The Heated Base is covered for two years, including the battery. Normal wear, dents from drops, and custom-coating scratches aren't covered, but replacement modules are always cheaper than replacement bottles. That's the point of the system.
