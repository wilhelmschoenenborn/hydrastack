How it works

The Tube Principle

Every section shares the same thread pitch. The lid screws onto the mid, the mid screws onto the base. Swap any piece, anytime.

Mid chamber
Base module
Universal threading

Every connection uses the same thread pitch. The lid screws onto the mid, the mid screws onto the base. You can even stack two mids together for double capacity.

Standard diameter 3.2"

The lid and mid chamber share a 3.2" outer diameter — a clean straight cylinder. This is your core tube that holds your drink.

Adaptive base width

The base module comes in standard (3.2") or narrow (2.5") diameter. A tapered adapter ring connects the narrow base to the mid — perfect for tight cupholders.
